rural development

alleviating rural poverty

At Sattva Sansthan, our commitment to rural development focuses on empowering communities and transforming lives in India’s villages. We believe that true progress can be achieved by addressing the unique needs of rural areas—through education, sustainable livelihood programs, healthcare initiatives, and infrastructure development. Our projects include skill development workshops, agricultural training, health camps, and initiatives to improve access to clean water and sanitation. By fostering local talent and enhancing resources, we strive to create self-sustaining communities that are resilient and prosperous. Sattva Sansthan works closely with community leaders and local organizations, ensuring that each project directly benefits the people it serves. Together, we are paving the way for a brighter future in rural India, one village at a time.

Updates

Sattva Sansthan is a registered non-profit and non-political organization under the Society Registration Act, 1958 (Rajasthan), dedicated to addressing the root causes of poverty and empowering vulnerable communities in India. Based in Jodhpur, our mission is to drive sustainable, positive change through targeted programs in education, healthcare, and livelihood support. Your contribution, no matter the amount, is invaluable to us and will directly support our initiatives in creating a brighter future for those in need.
