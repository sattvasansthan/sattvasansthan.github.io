who are we

Sattva Sansthan is a registered non-profit, non-political organization under the Society Registration Act, 1958 (Rajasthan), committed to tackling the root causes of poverty and uplifting vulnerable communities across India.

Operating from Jodhpur, our mission is to create sustainable, transformative change through focused programs in education, healthcare, and livelihood support, fostering resilience and self-reliance among those we serve.

Every contribution, regardless of size, is a powerful investment in the lives of individuals striving for a better tomorrow.

By supporting Sattva Sansthan, you’re not only helping provide immediate relief but also laying the groundwork for lasting impact.

Join us in building a future where opportunity, dignity, and hope are accessible to all. Together, let’s make a meaningful difference.

Vision, Mission and Aim

Our Vision

Our vision at Sattva Sansthan is to create an inclusive and empowered society where every individual has access to basic healthcare, quality education, and sustainable livelihood opportunities. We are dedicated to improving lives through specialized initiatives, such as our eye care programs, which provide essential vision care and awareness for those in need. By addressing fundamental challenges, we envision a future where underserved communities thrive with health, knowledge, and the skills needed to uplift themselves and contribute positively to society.

Our Mission

Our mission at Sattva Sansthan is to uplift vulnerable communities by providing essential support in healthcare, education, and sustainable livelihoods. We are dedicated to fostering positive change through programs that address immediate needs and empower individuals with knowledge and skills for long-term growth. Through our initiatives, such as vision camps, educational workshops, and skill-building programs, we aim to create self-sufficient communities and bridge gaps in access to resources, ultimately contributing to a more equitable and inclusive society.

Our Aim

Our aim at Sattva Sansthan is to drive meaningful, sustainable change for underserved communities by addressing critical needs in education, healthcare, and livelihood support. We strive to empower individuals with the resources and skills required to build a brighter, self-sufficient future. Through targeted initiatives, we aim to break the cycle of poverty, foster resilience, and promote well-being, creating a foundation for long-term community development and social transformation.
