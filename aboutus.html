About Us

Sattvasansthan is a Non Government & Non Profit organization since 2004 registered under Society Registration Act, 1958 at Jodhpur, Rajasthan (India). Your support will go a long way in helping us in our efforts. Any amount of money you give will be appreciated. Donate now to support our mission towards underlying problems of poverty in India.
