who are we

Sattva Sansthan is a non profit, non political NGO, registered under society registration act 1958 (RAJASTHAN) at jodhpur.

We execute development programs as well as major thrust on advocacy for child rights, women empowerment, good governance and transparency to make the society free from injustice, poverty, corruption and hunger.

We pay special attention for the capacity building and awareness of those invulnerable situations.

Our efforts on health care ensure accessible, affordable, available, and acceptable primary health care services to the most vulnerable and marginalized people in both rural and urban settings.

Our NGO organizes regular health check-up camps and has partnered with various other charities.

As one of the top-rated NGOs in Rajasthan, we investigate the underlying causes of healthcare issues and offer creative fixes for high-quality health care in India.

Our Aim

To create awareness regarding health and help everyone to achive that.

To provide educational guidance for the needy.

To assist students and graduates to avail job opportunities.

To help and assist rural area for better livelihood.

To provide guidance for availing financial assistance(loans, investment, etc ,..) in both public and private

To guide the public to get medical facilities on the right places on right time.

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a society where every individual has the opportunity to live a healthy and prosperous life. We aim to provide quality healthcare, education, and livelihood opportunities to the underprivileged sections of society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to work towards the upliftment of the underprivileged sections of society by providing them with access to quality healthcare, education, and livelihood opportunities. We aim to create a society where every individual has the opportunity to live a healthy and prosperous life.
