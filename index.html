what we do

Healthcare

Why we look for Heathcare

India has made rapid strides in the health sector since independence. However, various eye opening data from NFHS clearly indicate that access to healthcare still remains a challenge.

Education

Why we look for education

Sattva Sansthan is committed to providing basic education and healthcare to underprivileged children. Our NGO believes that whether you are addressing healthcare, poverty, population control, unemployment or human rights, there's no better place to start than in the corridors of education.

Revitalization

Why we look for Revitalization

The youth comprises over one-third of the Indian population which in turn constitutes a major part of the labour force of the country.The number of underprivileged youth in India who lacks education and proper guidance is so huge.

Rural Development

Why we look for rural Development

updates

Sattva Sansthan is a registered non-profit and non-political organization under the Society Registration Act, 1958 (Rajasthan), dedicated to addressing the root causes of poverty and empowering vulnerable communities in India. Based in Jodhpur, our mission is to drive sustainable, positive change through targeted programs in education, healthcare, and livelihood support. Your contribution, no matter the amount, is invaluable to us and will directly support our initiatives in creating a brighter future for those in need.
