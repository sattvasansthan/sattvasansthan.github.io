Sattva Sansthan is a registered non-profit and non-political organization under the Society Registration Act, 1958 (Rajasthan), dedicated to addressing the root causes of poverty and empowering vulnerable communities in India. Based in Jodhpur, our mission is to drive sustainable, positive change through targeted programs in education, healthcare, and livelihood support. Your contribution, no matter the amount, is invaluable to us and will directly support our initiatives in creating a brighter future for those in need.
